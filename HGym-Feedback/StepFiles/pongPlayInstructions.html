What to expect next:

You are about to play 3 full rounds (each round is decided by the first to 20 points) of Pong. A round will automatically end if it has not been finished after 4 minutes. The goal of the game is to bounce the ball past your opponents paddle to score goals without letting it pass by your paddle. To interact with the game use the up and down arrow keys.

Please play the game to the best of your ability.

Please click "Submit" when you're ready to move on, and round 1 of 3 will begin.
