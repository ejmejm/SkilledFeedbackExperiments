What to expect next:

You are about to play 3 full rounds (each round has 3 lives) of Mario. A round will automatically end if it has not been finished after 4 minutes. The goal of the game is to get the highest score possible by progressing to the right while collecting items and defeating enemies.

Controls

To interact with the game use the following keys:

  • left & right arrow - move
  • z key - jump
  • x key - use ability

Please play the game to the best of your ability.

Please click "Submit" when you're ready to move on, and round 1 of 3 will begin.
